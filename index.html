About Me

Hi! I'm Tom, a 16-year-old filmmaker based in Christchurch, New Zealand. I specialize in FPV drone filming for action sports like mountain biking, BMX, and freestyle events. In addition, I capture cinematic ground shots using normal cameras to complement the drone footage. My goal is to create visually stunning content that tells a complete story from every angle.
