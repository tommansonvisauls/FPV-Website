About Me

I’m Tom, a 16-year-old filmmaker based in Christchurch, New Zealand. I blend FPV drone agility with steady, cinematic ground shots to create fast, energetic visuals that feel intentional and alive.

My work focuses on action sports and high-intensity environments — MTB, BMX, Surron, and events. I fly FPV chase cams, shoot handheld and gimbal, and edit everything with a music-driven, story-first style.

Based in Christchurch. Available NZ-wide.
